About Me

Builder. Thinker. Connector.

I am a Product Manager with 10+ years of experience driving enterprise SaaS and IoT platforms across healthcare, genomics, and life sciences organizations. I have a demonstrated track record of leading smart hospital and clinical data platforms, integrating Location AI, predictive analytics, and data-driven decision support into real-world healthcare operations.

My academic foundation — an M.S. in Medical Informatics from Rutgers paired with a B.S. in Nutrition and Biology — gives me a rare ability to bridge the clinical and the technological. I've built on that with advanced work in Agentic AI Product Management, applied ML, and large-scale SaaS platform design.

Beyond product work, I host a podcast exploring the intersection of technology, human potential, and modern life. I also serve as the Community Lead of a Philosophy Club, where I facilitate conversations on ethics, consciousness, and the nature of decision-making — themes that deeply inform how I approach product thinking.
